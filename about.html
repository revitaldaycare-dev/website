About Revital Daycare

Our commitment to quality, care, and community

Our Story

Revital Daycare was founded by Revital Edry, a dedicated early childhood educator with a passion for nurturing young minds. Inspired by her belief that every child deserves a warm, enriching environment to grow, Revital set out to create a home away from home for Winnetka families. The name "Revital" reflects our mission: to revitalize the joy of learning, play, and community in every child we serve.

Today, Revital Daycare serves families across Winnetka and the surrounding community. We're proud of the trust parents place in us and the relationships we build with every child in our care.

Our Mission

To provide nurturing, high-quality childcare that supports each child's growth, fosters creativity and learning, and builds a warm community where families feel valued and heard.

Care

Every child receives individualized attention, compassion, and support in a safe, loving environment.

Learning

We foster curiosity and growth through play-based, developmentally appropriate activities.

Community

We build strong partnerships with families and celebrate diversity in our community.

Excellence

We maintain high standards of quality, safety, and professional practice.

Meet Our Team

Experienced, caring professionals dedicated to your child's growth

Revital Edry

Teacher

Revital is a dedicated early childhood educator with years of experience working with infants, toddlers, and preschoolers. She holds a degree in early childhood education and is CPR/First Aid certified. Revital created Revital Daycare to bring her vision of warm, play-based learning to Winnetka families.
