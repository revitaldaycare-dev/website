Welcome to Revital Daycare

Located in the heart of Winnetka, CA, Revital Daycare is a licensed home-based early childhood program serving families with children ages 6 weeks through 5 years. Founded by teacher Revital Edry, our program combines the warmth of a home environment with the structure and educational focus of a professional preschool.

We believe that the early years are the most formative, and we take that responsibility seriously. Our play-based, child-centered approach — inspired by Reggio Emilia principles — encourages children to explore, create, and discover at their own pace. Every activity in our daily routine is designed to support cognitive, social-emotional, physical, and language development.

With low child-to-staff ratios (3:1 for infants, 4:1 for toddlers, 6:1 for preschoolers), your child receives the individualized attention they deserve. We partner closely with parents through daily updates and open communication to ensure consistency between home and daycare.
