Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation?

We're open Monday through Friday, 6:30 AM to 6:00 PM. We're closed on weekends and major holidays.

What ages do you serve?

We serve children from 6 weeks through 5 years, with separate programs for infants, toddlers, and preschoolers.

How do I schedule a tour?

Contact us via phone, email, or the form above. We offer tours by appointment throughout the week.

What is your child-to-staff ratio?

Our ratios are 3:1 for infants, 4:1 for toddlers, and 6:1 for preschoolers. We maintain low child-to-staff ratios to ensure personalized attention.

Do you offer flexible scheduling?

Yes, we offer full-time (5 days/week) and part-time (3 days/week) enrollment. We also offer flexible drop-in care when space is available. Contact us to discuss which option works best for your schedule.

What's included in your curriculum?

Our programs include academics, arts, music, outdoor play, and social-emotional learning tailored to each age group.
